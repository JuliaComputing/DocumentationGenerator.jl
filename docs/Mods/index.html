    Mods.Mod — Type.

    Mod(v,m) creates a modular number in mod m with value v%m. Mod(m) is equivalent to Mod(0,m).

    Base.inv — Method.

    inv(x::Mod) gives the multiplicative inverse of x. This may be abbreviated by x'.

    Mods.CRT — Method.

    CRT(m1,m2,...): Chinese Remainder Theorem

    julia> CRT( Mod(4,11), Mod(8,14) )
Mods.Mod(92,154)

julia> 92%11
4

julia> 92%14
8
